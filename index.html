Our Mission

This project examines the effects of small tubercles on propeller blade leading edges. We have adapted this idea to variable pitch propellers to determine how tubercles affect their performance. Blade geometries are modelled in Fusion 360 and fabricated using 3D printers for prototype testing.

What We Are Currently Working On
  • Integrating simulation results with the physical propeller prototype for validation
  • Developing and iterating on the latest propeller blade geometry in Fusion 360
  • Refining tubercle placement and geometry on blade leading edges to accurately measure their aerodynamic effect
