Our Mission

Our mission in this project is to investigate and examine the effects of the small tubercles on the leading edge of the propeller blades. We also adapted the idea of tuburcles to variable pitch propellers, aiming to determine their effects on variable pitch propellers. In this project, we combine biomimicry and propellers in order to enhance the propeller design and achieve more efficient propellers. We are designing the propeller from Fusion 360 and printing it with 3D printers.

What We are Currently Working On

  • ▻ In our last prototype test, the propeller broke due to the thin and durable structure of the propeller housing. To solve this problem, we are working on strengthening the propeller housing design and we switched from PLA to PETG filament in order to ensure that our new propeller design is stronger when compared to the old version
  • ▻ We are working on the electronics of our design where we try to ensure that the step motor has microstepping and works properly
  • ▻ We are working on the propeller blade designs in order to accurately examine the tuburcle effect
