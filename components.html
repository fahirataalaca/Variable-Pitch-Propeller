Key Components

The following are the primary components used in the variable pitch propeller project:

  • Stepper Motor (NEMA 17 HS4401) - The primary actuator for the propeller's pitch adjustment.
  • DRV8825 Driver - A microstepping driver used to control the stepper motor.
  • Li-ion Batteries - Compact, rechargeable power source for the motor and electronics.
  • Arduino Uno - The microcontroller responsible for controlling the stepper motor.
  • 3D-Printed Propeller - The propeller blades and housing designed using Fusion 360 and printed with PETG for durability.

Additional Components

In addition to the core components, the project also includes sensors, wiring, and a control circuit to ensure smooth operation during testing. These components help facilitate proper communication between the system and ensure optimal functioning of the propeller during flight or wind tunnel tests.
